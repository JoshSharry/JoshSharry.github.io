Beautiful youth

Beautiful Youth

Deliverables
Film Titles, Packaging

This project was a live project where we paired
up with the film students to design their film titles. The film I got was Beautiful Youth which was about a girl who suffered from a mental illness called cycosis. My concept derived from this and I created a visual language around
nerves flashes in the brain of her thoughts.

For my packaging I decided to do a prescribed medication look. This idea came after I had
researched into cycosis which you take prescribed medication for and decided
to present the film with this vibe.
