For this project we were put into groups
of two and given a brief to choose. We chose
the RSA “Sleep Matters” Campaign brief.
For this we focused on how technology can
at night time decrease sleep duration but
also can decrease libido with your partner. Sextember is a campaign created to increase your sex life and therefore increase the
amount of sleep you are getting.
